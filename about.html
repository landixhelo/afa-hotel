ABOUT US

Enjoy Your Vacation In Our Hotel And Get Lots Of Fun, Happiness And Great Pleasure

-Set within 100 metres of Durres Beach and 38 km of Skanderbeg Square, AFA Hotel offers rooms with air conditioning and a private bathroom in Durrës. With free WiFi, this 3-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk. The property is non-smoking and is located 42 km from Dajti Ekspres Cable Car.

-At the hotel, all rooms come with a desk, a flat-screen TV, a private bathroom, bed linen and towels. AFA Hotel provides certain units with city views, and every room includes a balcony. The units feature a wardrobe. Guests at the accommodation can enjoy a continental breakfast.

-Rock of Kavaje is 7.3 km from AFA Hotel, while Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is 38 km away. The nearest airport is Tirana International Mother Teresa Airport, 34 km from the hotel.
